This website is about Sustainable Cities!

Sustainable cities are cities that are more environmentally friendly. This can be by being self-sufficient in energy, better public transport, green spaces that are maintained well, and waste being used to produce energy. Sustainable cities are part of the United Nations 17 sustainability goals.
