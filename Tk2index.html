20-minute neighbourhood

By Titas kelevisius

A 20-Minute neighbourhood is a sustainable way of living where
everyone in a community should be a 20 minute walk/cycle away
from their essentials. this improves residents healt, well being and
allows for a more social community. This also is great for the
environment because it removes the dependancy of cars.
