SMART COMMUTING

Nowadays, people have a number of ways to get around- walking, flying, cycling, driving, commuting via bus, boat or train. As we should. However. All these modes of transport just to get to your job has the disastrous result of emitting quite a lot of CO2 into the air, and our earth’s atmosphere is suffering. Back in 2017, Interreg Europe had a wonderful idea of how to cut back on our CO2 emissions- and a whopping 7 out of 195 countries on our atmosphere-suffering earth agreed. And it was only parts of Italy. Not the whole country. 6.5 countries. In fairness it has “Europe” in the title so it’s not like the Americas were gonna care.
