Vertical forests

By Titas kelevisius

Vertical forests are forests that are built upwards using buildings and
infastructure as support. They are built to give the impression that citys
have living buildings that look appealing and have numerous ecological
benefits. The idea of living buildings is that they can breathe, cool
themselves and contrabute to its surroundings in posative ways. vertical
buildings allow environmental regeneration and urban regeneration without
needing more space for forests.

Positives of vertical farming

  • Cleaner air

  • More beutiful cities

  • Creates homes for animals

  • More biodiversity

  • Reduces CO2 emissions
